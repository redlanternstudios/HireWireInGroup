# Meetings & Notes Commands
Commands for capturing, cleaning, and acting on meeting content.

/MEETINGNOTES [transcript] — Clean notes. Key decisions, action items, open questions.
/ACTIONS [notes] — Extract all action items. Owner, task, deadline for each.
/STANDUP [yesterday + today + blockers] — Clean standup update. Three sections, no fluff.
/RECAP [meeting] — Meeting recap. What was decided, what's next, who owns what.
/DECISIONS [notes] — Every decision made. What was decided and why.
/QUESTIONS [topic] — Questions to bring to a meeting. Ones that move things forward.
/STATUS [project] — Project status update. Where we are, what's next, what's blocked.
/BRIEFME [topic + paste] — Pre-brief on this topic. What I need to know to add value.
/DEBRIEF [meeting] — What went well, what didn't, what to do differently.
/TLDR [paste] — 3-sentence TL;DR of this content.
/RETRO [project] — Retrospective. What worked, what didn't, what we carry forward.
