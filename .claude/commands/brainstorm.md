# Brainstorm Commands
Commands for generating ideas quickly and broadly.

/IDEAS20 [topic] — 20 ideas. Range from obvious to wild. No filtering.
/GIFTS [person + occasion + budget] — 10 specific gift ideas.
/NAMES [thing + vibe] — 20 name ideas. Include domain-friendly options.
/UNUSUAL [problem] — 5 non-conventional approaches. No standard answers.
/ANGLE [topic] — Overlooked angles. The takes nobody is writing about.
/COMBINE [A + B] — Interesting ideas from combining these two things.
/METAPHOR [concept] — 5 metaphors that explain this in a different way.
/STARTERS [topic] — 10 non-boring conversation or content starters.
/JOURNAL10 [theme] — 10 journal prompts that lead to real insight.
/AS [goal] — Approach as a world-class expert. What would they do?
/CHILD [problem] — How would a curious 8-year-old solve this?
