# Email & Messages Commands
Commands for drafting, editing, and responding to emails and messages.

/DECLINE [email] — Write a polite, professional refusal. Warm, firm, brief. No over-explaining.
/SHORTEN [email] — Cut to half the length. Keep the ask. Remove everything else.
/WARMER [email] — Rewrite to feel less stiff. Same message, drop the corporate tone.
/FOLLOWUP [topic] — Write a follow-up. Assume no response. Direct, not desperate.
/ANSWER [request] — Clear, direct response. One ask, one answer. No filler.
/BULLETPROOF [outlets] — Rewrite so it cannot be misread, ignored, or deflected.
/THANKS [person] — Genuine, specific thank you. Reference what they actually did.
/COMFORT [email] — Supportive, empathetic reply. Warm tone. No empty phrases.
/OOO [dates + backup] — Out-of-office auto-reply. Include backup contact.
/HARDSIG [topic] — Strong, confident closing paragraph. Makes people act.
/INTRO [person A + person B] — Mutual introduction email. Why they should know each other.
