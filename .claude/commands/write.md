# Write & Edit Commands
Commands for improving, rewriting, and refining any text.

/PROOF [text] — Fix typos, grammar, punctuation. Do not change voice or meaning.
/REWRITES [sentence] — 5 different versions. Range from formal to casual.
/CLARIFY [text] — Rewrite to be clearer. Same message, easier to understand.
/OPENERS [text] — 5 stronger opening lines. Hook-first, no throat-clearing.
/FILLER [text] — Flag every filler word and weak phrase. Show what to cut.
/ACTIVE [text] — Rewrite in active voice throughout. No passive constructions.
/HUMAN [text] — Rewrite so a real person wrote it. Remove all AI-isms.
/EXAMPLES [text] — Add concrete examples to every claim. No unsupported assertions.
/SIMPLIFY [text] — Rewrite at 12th-grade reading level. Keep the meaning.
/TITLE [text] — 10 title options. Range from direct to curiosity-driven.
/TONE [text + vibe] — Rewrite to match this specific tone. Keep content, change feel.
