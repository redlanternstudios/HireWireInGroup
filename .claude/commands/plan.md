# Plan & Organize Commands
Commands for planning, scheduling, and organizing work.

/WEEK [priorities] — Build week plan from priorities. Block time, sequence tasks.
/MILESTONES [goal + deadline] — Break goal into milestones. Work backward from deadline.
/PACK [trip] — Complete packing list. Every category covered.
/SCHEDULE [event + duration] — Detailed timed agenda. Every block has a purpose.
/ROUTINE [goal] — Daily routine built around this goal. Realistic and sustainable.
/PRIORITIZE [list] — Reorder by true priority. Highest impact first. Explain ranking.
/MEALS [ingredients + constraints] — Week of meals from these ingredients.
/AGENDA [topic + duration] — Meeting agenda. Every item has owner and time.
/PREPARE [event + time] — Prep checklist for this event. Most important items first.
/ORDER [tasks] — Sequence tasks optimally. Dependencies first, quick wins early.
/CHECKLIST [task] — Complete step-by-step checklist. Nothing assumed, nothing skipped.
