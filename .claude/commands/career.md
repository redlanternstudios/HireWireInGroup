# Work & Career Commands
Commands for career development and professional communication.

/INTERVIEWQ [questions] — Confident, specific answers grounded in real examples.
/RESUMEBULLET [outlet] — Stronger resume bullet. Impact first, evidence second.
/ASKINTERVIEWER [role + company type] — Smart questions that show deep thinking.
/NEGOTIATE [role] — Salary negotiation script. Confident, non-apologetic.
/RECONNECT [person + context] — Warm outreach to reconnect. Specific, not transactional.
/GAP [reason] — Professional explanation for resume gap. Honest, forward-focused.
/WINS [roles] — Turn responsibilities into accomplishment statements.
/ONEONONE [topic] — Prep for 1:1 with manager. What to say, ask, watch for.
/SELFREVIEW [paste] — Self-review from this work. Specific, quantified, confident.
/IDIOMTOOL [situation] — Right phrase for this professional situation.
/RAISE [context] — Script to ask for a raise. Specific, calm, outcome-focused.
