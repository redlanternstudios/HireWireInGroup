AI Product Manager

Title: AI Product Manager

Company: SignalWorks AI

Location: Remote, United States

Salary: 145000 to 170000

Job Description

SignalWorks AI is seeking an AI Product Manager to lead the design, delivery, and optimization of AI powered workflow products for enterprise customers. This role partners closely with engineering, design, data, customer success, and executive stakeholders to translate customer problems into clear product requirements and measurable roadmap priorities.

Responsibilities:

  1. Own roadmap strategy for AI powered workflow products.
  2. Translate user needs and enterprise customer pain points into product requirements, user stories, and acceptance criteria.
  3. Partner with engineering teams working with APIs, cloud platforms, LLMs, and data systems.
  4. Define KPIs to evaluate feature success, adoption, workflow efficiency, and user trust.
  5. Design safe AI experiences using guardrails, evaluation loops, and human centered UX patterns.
  6. Collaborate with GTM and customer facing teams to align product delivery with market needs.
  7. Lead cross functional delivery in Agile environments.
  8. Communicate product decisions and tradeoffs to technical and non technical stakeholders.

Required Qualifications:

  1. 4+ years of product management or technical product experience.
  2. Experience delivering AI, automation, workflow, or data products.
  3. Strong understanding of APIs, cloud platforms, and software delivery.
  4. Experience writing requirements, user stories, and acceptance criteria.
  5. Strong stakeholder management and cross functional leadership.
  6. Experience defining KPIs and using data to guide product decisions.
  7. Excellent written and verbal communication.

Preferred Qualifications:

  1. Experience with LLMs, prompt design, model evaluation, or AI safety.
  2. Experience with enterprise platforms such as Salesforce, SAP, or similar systems.
  3. Experience with SQL, analytics tools, or dashboarding.
  4. Experience in startup or consulting environments.
  5. Experience with GTM alignment and customer discovery.
